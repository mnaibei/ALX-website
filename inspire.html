Be Part Of The Rising


Take The Lead


Africa needs her Youth to be leaders and not only followers, as a young African, you need to take an interest in your community welfare and let the change you want to see start with you. Be the one to improve your community, contribute something and don't wait around for someone else to get the job done.

Be A Mentor


Mentorship plays a crucial part in the motivation of uprising young Africans, in whatever field or area of knowledge you have excelled in or better at, find someone to mentor and be able to provide encouragement and guidance to them. This helps build the self-confidence of the ones being mentored as they feel accepted aand supported.

Inspire


Share your community success stories, inspire and challenge others through your works. Every breakthrough has potential to spark interest in others. As a torch bearer, it is your duty to ensure the flame is shared and passed on. Success stories are able to encourage those striving towards their dreams, and help them to keep believing that it is actually possible to achieve the “impossible”.
